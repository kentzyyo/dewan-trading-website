Established on July 2008, Dewan Trading was created to cater to the needs of institutions in terms of medical and industrial gases. The company initiated extensive strategic and hard work marketing and sales movement until it was able to locally compete with the other players in the market. Within a year, it was able to introduce its name to the Bicol Region.

Dewan Trading specializes in provision of quality products such as Oxygen (Medical), Nitrogen, Argon, Hydrogen, and many more to our valued customers in the field of health care and engineering. Within its few years of operation, Dewan has managed to earn the trust and loyalty of various institutions and its people, managing to get a wide variety of customers all over Bicol Region.
