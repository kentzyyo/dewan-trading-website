Medical and Industrial Gases

Discover our comprehensive range of high-quality gases for medical and industrial applications, backed by industry-leading expertise and service.

OXYGEN

Type: Liquid & Gas
Purity: 99.9%

Oxygen (O₂) is a colorless, odorless gas essential for life and various industrial processes.

Key Applications:

  • Medical: Life support systems, hospitals
  • Industrial: Metal cutting, welding
  • Chemical manufacturing
  • Water treatment

Safety: Store in a well-ventilated area away from combustible materials.

NITROGEN

Type: Liquid & Gas
Purity: 99.999%

Nitrogen (N₂) is an inert gas that makes up about 78% of Earth's atmosphere.

Applications:

  • Food packaging and preservation
  • Electronics manufacturing
  • Chemical blanketing and purging
  • Laser cutting and welding

Safety: Store in a well-ventilated area away from combustible materials.

ARGON

Type: Liquid & Gas
Purity: 99.999%

Argon (Ar) is a noble gas that is colorless, odorless, and non-reactive.

Applications:

  • Welding and metal fabrication
  • Lighting (neon signs)
  • Semiconductor manufacturing
  • Window insulation

Safety: Store in a well-ventilated area away from combustible materials.

HYDROGEN

Type: Gas
   

Hydrogen (H₂) is a colorless, odorless, and highly flammable gas.

Applications:

  • Fuel cells
  • Chemical synthesis
  • Metal fabrication

Safety: Store in a well-ventilated area away from combustible materials.

ACETYLENE

Type: Gas
   

Acetylene (C₂H₂) is a colorless, odorless, and highly flammable gas.

Applications:

  • Welding and metal fabrication
  • Chemical synthesis
  • Lighting (acetylene torches)

Safety: Store in a well-ventilated area away from combustible materials.

ARCOWELD (Argoshield)

Type: Gas
   

Arcoweld is a type of welding process that uses argon gas as a shielding gas.

Applications:

  • Welding and metal fabrication
  • Automotive manufacturing
  • Aerospace manufacturing

Safety: Store in a well-ventilated area away from combustible materials.

CARBON DIOXIDE

Type: Gas

Carbon Dioxide (CO₂) is a colorless, odorless gas that is essential for life on Earth.

Key Applications:

  • Beverage carbonation
  • Food packaging and preservation
  • Greenhouse enrichment
  • Fire extinguishers
  • Industrial cleaning

Safety: Use in well-ventilated areas. Can displace oxygen in confined spaces.

NITROUS OXIDE

Type: Gas

Nitrous Oxide (N₂O) is a colorless gas with a slightly sweet odor, also known as "laughing gas".

Key Applications:

  • Medical and dental anesthesia
  • Food industry (whipped cream propellant)
  • Automotive industry (engine performance)
  • Laboratory research

Safety: Medical-grade gas requiring proper handling and storage. Use only under professional supervision.
