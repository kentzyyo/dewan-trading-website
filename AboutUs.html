COMPANY BACKGROUND

Established on July 2008, Dewan Trading was created to cater the needs of institutions in terms of medical and industrial gases. The company initiated extensive strategic and hard work marketing and sales movement until it was able to locally compete with the other players in the market. Within a year, it was able to introduce its name to the Bicol Region.

Dewan Trading specializes in provision of quality products such as Oxygen (Medical), Nitrogen, Argon, Hydrogen, and many more to our valued customers in the field of health care and engineering. Within its few years of operation, Dewan has managed to earn the trust and loyalty of various institutions and its people, managing to get a wide variety of customers all over Bicol Region.

OUR HISTORY

2010

2011

To serve the customers promptly and to attain precision on the deliveries, a LIQUID OXYGEN PUMPING STATION was renovated, a facility that can cater sufficiency of supply to all its customers for the whole Bicol Region, including Northern Samar.

2012

Opening of branches and outlets at Sorsogon, Camarines Sur and Albay.
